The Association for Women in Computing strives to

promote inclusivity in STEM by giving underrepresented  

genders and allies in computing the platform to exceed.

We partner with companies to bring Tech Talks, career

opportunities and professional contacts to our members.

Additionally, we host social events to connect our members

and allow them to celebrate their diversity and shared

experiences. Our goal is to foster a supportive community

within the computing majors on campus and encourage

minorities to continue to pursue computing.
